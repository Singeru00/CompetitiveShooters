Apex Legends is a battle royale game developed by Response Entertainment and published by EA . It was released for Microsoft Windows , PlayStation 4 and Xbox One on February 4, 2019, with no prior announcement or marketing.

The game combines elements from various video games; battle royale games, class shooters and those with evolving narratives. Approximately sixty players face off against each other on the island in teams of three, with one player controlling where their squad is located. Teams then have to collect weapons and other resources to fight against other squads, the playing space gradually shrinks until one team is left, ending the match. The game also features the ability to revive your teammates over a period of time. Communication with team members can be done via voice chat or via a ping system, allowing teammates to tag supplies, locations and enemies with ease. The game has nine heroes to choose from (although three of them are locked) and have a microtransaction system for cosmetic items.
